CARTOON
modern medias worst crime is that it has cartoonized and trivialized power. this is fine when it is framed comedically (as in Looney Toons) or when the dissonance is played for laughs; it is downright insidious when it is not (as in YA and marvel). people and children especially have a developed a distorted understand of great strength or bravery or intellect as a thing with which one is endowed or not endowed rather than something that one works to obtain and express.
