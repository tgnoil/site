VACCINE
sickness is an opening up and a coming in, perversely. The well may employ the sick function as Vaccine, or, a re-versioning of the perverse (not a cancellation of the perverse but a multiplication of it). 
When power wields the means of vaccination, then should the well fear. For that which is vaccinated against may be the very thing the Body needs most. What is learned is this: that to be well is to have been sick. 
When the body rejects the foreign substance, it rejects a becoming, for good or ill. When other than the self holds sway over this becoming, then should the self fear. 
But does not the World hold sway over the self, to introduce or withhold? No. For world demonstrably is self and self world. Seizure of the world-self and containment of its workings are figured as: the Tube or Needle, forcefully inserted.
What to do, we who are sufferers of the real and imagined? Find joy in the foreign body as in the body of the self, forsaking fear of fear: intubated Other.
The victim, friend, is no one and the death toll but One.
